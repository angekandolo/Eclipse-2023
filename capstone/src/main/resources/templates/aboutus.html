About MangaUpdates

MangaUpdates (also known as Baka-Updates Manga) is a dedicated platform aimed at providing the manga community, including the manhwa, manhua, and other related communities, with the most up-to-date scanlation and series information. Our journey began in July 2004, and we proudly stand as the sister site of Baka-Updates.

Piracy and Copyright Infringement Statement

MangaUpdates strictly adheres to the principles of respecting copyright laws and opposes any form of piracy. Our platform is not designed to facilitate the unauthorized transfer of copyrighted material between users. Instead, our primary objective is to serve as a valuable source of information for manga series and create a welcoming community for our users. Additionally, we function as an index, providing insights into activities within the manga scanlation community to those interested. There are no direct links to torrents, zip files, images, or any other infringing content on our website.

Privacy

At MangaUpdates, we take your privacy seriously. Any information shared through private messages or communications with our staff will be treated with the utmost confidentiality and will not be disseminated to any third party. Furthermore, personal information, such as email addresses, will undergo scrutiny upon registration to safeguard against spam associations. However, rest assured that we will not share such information with any third parties without your consent.

We are committed to providing an exceptional platform for manga enthusiasts while maintaining respect for creators' rights and the privacy of our users. Thank you for being a part of the MangaUpdates community. Happy reading!
